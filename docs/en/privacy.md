# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This privacy policy explains how OpenCode Chinese Tutorial (learnopencode.com, hereinafter referred to as "this site") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information Collection

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this site, we may automatically collect the following information through third-party services:

- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **Visit Information**: Visit time, page view history, referring website
- **Network Information**: IP address (used for approximate geographic location)

### Cookies and Similar Technologies

This site uses cookies and similar technologies to:

- Remember your preference settings (such as dark/light theme)
- Analyze website traffic and usage
- Display relevant advertisements

## Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

### Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your interests.

- [Google Ads Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
- [How to manage Google ad settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)

### Google Analytics (if enabled)

This site may use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic.

- [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Advertising Notice

All tutorial content on this site is completely free. We display advertisements to cover server and domain costs.

- Advertisements are provided by Google AdSense
- Ad content is automatically matched by Google based on your interests
- You can manage personalized ad preferences through [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)

## Your Choices

### Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings:

- [Chrome Cookie Settings](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox Cookie Settings](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari Cookie Settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge Cookie Settings](https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

### Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized ads through:

- [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative Opt-out Page](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)

## Children's Privacy

This site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, please note that internet transmission cannot be 100% secure.

## Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:

- GitHub Issues: [https://github.com/vbgate/learn-opencode/issues](https://github.com/vbgate/learn-opencode/issues)
- WeChat Group: [Join Community](/en/community)
